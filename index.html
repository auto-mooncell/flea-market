Green Fair Flea Market

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle: Give Your Items a Second Life

Event Details

Date: April 25, 2025 (Friday)

Time: 12:00 - 16:00

Location: NYUSH Campus Quad Area

Note: Alternative location will be announced in case of inclement weather

OFFICIAL PARTICIPATION RULES

Registration & Setup

  • All participants must complete registration through the Student Affairs Office or via the official online form by April 20, 2025.
  • Upon registration confirmation, participants are required to join the designated WeChat group for important announcements and updates.
  • Vendors must arrive between 10:30-11:30 AM on April 25 to complete booth setup before the 12:00 PM opening.
  • Each vendor will be allocated one standard table space (approximately 120cm x 60cm).
  • Participants must provide their own tablecloth and display materials/stands.
  • No electricity will be provided; please ensure electronic displays are fully charged.

During the Event

  • Vendors must maintain their assigned space throughout the duration of the event (12:00-16:00).
  • Price tags should be clearly displayed on all items.
  • Vendors are responsible for all transactions and securing their valuables.
  • All participants must adhere to university conduct policies throughout the event.

Post-Event Procedures

  • Vendors must clear their space by 16:30.
  • For unsold items, vendors must choose one of the following options:
    1. Collect all unsold items by 16:30
    2. Designate items for donation (must be clearly labeled with "DONATE IF UNSOLD" tags provided at check-in)
  • Important: Items not collected by 16:30 and not marked for donation will automatically be considered donations.
  • All donation recipients will receive electronic donation certificates for their records.

COMPREHENSIVE ITEM GUIDELINES

Permitted Items

  • Pre-owned books, textbooks, and academic materials
  • Gently used electronics in working condition (must be tested before the event)
  • Household items and decorations (in clean, functional condition)
  • Clothing and accessories (must be clean, unstained, and in good repair)
  • Handmade crafts and upcycled items
  • Sporting equipment and musical instruments (in working order)
  • Unused cosmetics and toiletries (unopened only)

Prohibited Items

STRICTLY PROHIBITED

  • New/unopened commercial products purchased specifically for resale
  • Counterfeit or replica designer items
  • Items that violate copyright or trademark laws
  • Food items without proper licensing
  • Heavily worn, stained, or damaged clothing
  • Broken or non-functional electronics
  • Prescription medications or controlled substances
  • Inappropriate imagery or content
  • Hazardous materials or chemicals
  • Personal hygiene items (used)
  • Items prohibited by university policy

Quality Standards

  • All items must be genuinely pre-owned and no longer needed by the seller
  • Electronics must be in working condition with minimum 50% battery life remaining
  • Clothing must be laundered, wrinkle-free, and without significant wear
  • Books should be free of extensive highlighting or damage
  • All items should be priced at 30-70% below retail value, reflecting their used condition

Sustainability Focus

  • Items that promote sustainability will be given priority booth placement
  • All packaging should be minimized or eliminated where possible
  • Sellers are encouraged to provide information about an item's history and usage

IMPORTANT NOTICE

The Event Committee reserves the right to inspect all items and request removal of any that do not comply with these guidelines. Non-compliance may result in exclusion from this event.

Event Purpose

  • Promote recycling and reuse on campus
  • Platform for graduating students to handle idle items
  • Reduce moving-out burden at the end of May
  • Raise environmental awareness
  • Create cross-cultural exchange opportunities

Donation & Environmental Initiatives

  • Unsold items can be donated to DSC for charity activities
  • Electronic donation certificates provided
  • Bring your own shopping bags
  • Electronic payment methods encouraged
  • Follow waste sorting regulations
